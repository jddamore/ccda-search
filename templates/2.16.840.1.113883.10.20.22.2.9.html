Assessment and Plan Section (V2) [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.9, release 2014-06-09] view pdf

Description:

This section represents the clinician's conclusions and working assumptions that will guide treatment of the patient. The Assessment and Plan Section may be combined or separated to meet local policy requirements.See also the Assessment Section: templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.8 and Plan of Treatment Section (V2): templateId 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.10

Contained By: Contains:
Consultation Note (V3)
History and Physical (V3)
Transfer Summary (V2)
Referral Note (V2)
Progress Note (V3)
Procedure Note (V3)
 Planned Act (V2)
