Medication Supply Order (V2) [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.17, release 2014-06-09] view pdf

Description:

This template records the intent to supply a patient with medications.

Contained By: Contains:
Medication Activity (V2)
Medication Dispense (V2)
Immunization Activity (V3)
 Medication Information (V2)
Immunization Medication Information (V2)
Instruction (V2)

Conformance Statements:

  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="SPLY" (CodeSystem: HL7ActClass 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 STATIC) (CONF:1098-7427).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode="INT" (CodeSystem: HL7ActMood 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1001 STATIC) (CONF:1098-7428).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId (CONF:1098-7429) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.17" (CONF:1098-10507).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09" (CONF:1098-32578).
  • SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF:1098-7430).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-7432).
    • This statusCode SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @code, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet ActStatus 2.16.840.1.113883.1.11.15933 DYNAMIC (CONF:1098-32362).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-15143) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] high (CONF:1098-15144).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] repeatNumber (CONF:1098-7434).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] quantity (CONF:1098-7436).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product (CONF:1098-7439) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Medication Information (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23) (CONF:1098-16093).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product (CONF:1098-9334) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54) (CONF:1098-31695).

      • A supply act SHALL contain one product/Medication Information OR one product/Immunization Medication Information template (CONF:1098-16870).

  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] author (CONF:1098-7438).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] entryRelationship (CONF:1098-7442).
    • The entryRelationship, if present, SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="SUBJ" (CodeSystem: HL7ActRelationshipType 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1002 STATIC) (CONF:1098-7444).
    • The entryRelationship, if present, SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @inversionInd="true" True (CONF:1098-7445).
    • The entryRelationship, if present, SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Instruction (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.20) (CONF:1098-31391).
