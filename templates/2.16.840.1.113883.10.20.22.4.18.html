Medication Dispense (V2) [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.18, release 2014-06-09] view pdf

Description:

This template records the act of supplying medications (i.e., dispensing).

Contained By: Contains:
Medication Activity (V2)
Immunization Activity (V3)
 Medication Information (V2)
Immunization Medication Information (V2)
US Realm Address (AD.US.FIELDED)
Medication Supply Order (V2)

Conformance Statements:

  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="SPLY" (CodeSystem: HL7ActClass 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 STATIC) (CONF:1098-7451).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode="EVN" (CodeSystem: HL7ActMood 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1001 STATIC) (CONF:1098-7452).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId (CONF:1098-7453) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.18" (CONF:1098-10505).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09" (CONF:1098-32580).
  • SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF:1098-7454).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-7455).
    • This statusCode SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @code, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Medication Fill Status 2.16.840.1.113883.3.88.12.80.64 STATIC 2014-04-23 (CONF:1098-32361).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-7456).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] repeatNumber (CONF:1098-7457).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] quantity (CONF:1098-7458).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product (CONF:1098-7459) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Medication Information (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.23) (CONF:1098-15607).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] product (CONF:1098-9331) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Immunization Medication Information (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.54) (CONF:1098-31696).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] performer (CONF:1098-7461).
    • The performer, if present, SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedEntity (CONF:1098-7467).
      • This assignedEntity SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] US Realm Address (AD.US.FIELDED) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.5.2) (CONF:1098-7468).

        • The content of addr SHALL be a conformant US Realm Address (AD.US.FIELDED) (2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.5.2) (CONF:1098-10565).

  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] entryRelationship (CONF:1098-7473) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="REFR" (CodeSystem: HL7ActRelationshipType 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1002 STATIC) (CONF:1098-7474).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Medication Supply Order (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.17) (CONF:1098-15606).

  • A supply act SHALL contain one product/Medication Information OR one product/Immunization Medication Information template (CONF:1098-9333).
