Physician Reading Study Performer (V2) [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.6.2.1, release 2014-06-09] view pdf

Description:

This participant is the Physician Reading Study Performer defined in documentationOf/serviceEvent. It is usually different from the attending physician. The reading physician interprets the images and evidence of the study (DICOM Definition).

Contained By: Contains:
Diagnostic Imaging Report (V3)
 US Realm Date and Time (DT.US.FIELDED)

Conformance Statements:

  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="PRF" Performer (CodeSystem: HL7ActClass 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 STATIC) (CONF:1098-8424).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId (CONF:1098-30773).
    • This templateId SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.6.2.1" (CONF:1098-30774).
    • This templateId SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09" (CONF:1098-32564).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] US Realm Date and Time (DT.US.FIELDED) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.5.3) (CONF:1098-8425).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedEntity (CONF:1098-8426).
    • This assignedEntity SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF:1098-10033).
    • This assignedEntity SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1098-8427).

      • SHALL contain a valid DICOM personal identification code sequence (@codeSystem is 1.2.840.10008.2.16.4) or an appropriate national health care provider coding system (e.g., NUCC in the U.S., where @codeSystem is 2.16.840.1.113883.6.101) (CONF:1098-8428).

    • Every assignedEntity element SHALL contain at least one [1..*] assignedPerson or representedOrganization (CONF:1098-8429).

    • The id SHOULD include zero or one [0..1] id where id/@root="2.16.840.1.113883.4.6" National Provider Identifier (CONF:1098-32135).
