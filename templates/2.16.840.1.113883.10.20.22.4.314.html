Latex Safety Observation [observation, 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.314, release 2019-06-21, open] view pdf

Description:

This template is intended to be used in addition to the Product Instance template 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.37 to exchange the Latex Safety Status of the patient's medical device. The UDI-DI of the medical device may be used to retrieve the Latex Safety status in accessGUDID, which should be considered the source of truth.
