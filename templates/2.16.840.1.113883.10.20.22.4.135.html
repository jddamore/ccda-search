Medical Equipment Organizer [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.135] view pdf

Description:

This template represents a set of current or historical medical devices, supplies, aids and equipment used by the patient. Examples are hearing aids, orthotic devices, ostomy supplies, visual aids, diabetic supplies such as syringes and pumps, and wheelchairs.Devices that are applied during a procedure (e.g., cardiac pacemaker, gastrosomy tube, port catheter), whether permanent or temporary, are represented within the Procedure Activity Procedure (V2) template (templateId: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.14.2).

Contained By: Contains:
Medical Equipment Section (V2)
 Procedure Activity Procedure (V2)
Non-Medicinal Supply Activity (V2)

Conformance Statements:

  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="CLUSTER" (CodeSystem: HL7ActClass 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 STATIC) (CONF:1098-31020).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode="EVN" Event (CodeSystem: HL7ActMood 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1001 STATIC) (CONF:1098-31021).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId (CONF:1098-31022) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.135" (CONF:1098-31023).
  • SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF:1098-31024).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] code (CONF:1098-31025).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-31026).
    • This statusCode SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @code, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Result Status 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.39 STATIC 2014-09-01 (CONF:1098-31029).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-32136).
    • This effectiveTime SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] low (CONF:1098-32378).
    • This effectiveTime SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] high (CONF:1098-32379).
  • MAY contain zero or more [0..*] component (CONF:1098-31027) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Non-Medicinal Supply Activity (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.50) (CONF:1098-31862).
  • MAY contain zero or more [0..*] component (CONF:1098-31887) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Procedure Activity Procedure (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.14) (CONF:1098-31888).

  • Either Non-Medicinal Supply Activity (V2) (templateId:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.50) OR Procedure Activity Procedure (V2) (templateId:2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.14) SHALL be present (CONF:1098-32380).
