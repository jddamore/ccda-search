Family History Section (V3) [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.15, release 2015-08-01] view pdf

Description:

This section contains data defining the patient's genetic relatives in terms of possible or relevant health risk factors that have a potential impact on the patient's healthcare risk profile.

Contained By: Contains:
Consultation Note (V3)
Continuity of Care Document (CCD) (V3)
Discharge Summary (V3)
History and Physical (V3)
Transfer Summary (V2)
Referral Note (V2)
Procedure Note (V3)
 Family History Organizer (V3)

    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.2.15" (CONF:1198-10388).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2015-08-01" (CONF:1198-32607).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] code (CONF:1198-15469).

  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] title (CONF:1198-7934).

    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Family History Organizer (V3) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.45) (CONF:1198-32431).
