Manufacturing Date Observation [observation, 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.316, release 2019-06-21, open] view pdf

Description:

This template is intended to be used in addition to the Product Instance template 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.37 to exchange the Manufacturing Date of the device. The manufacturing date is parsed from the UDI value, if present.
