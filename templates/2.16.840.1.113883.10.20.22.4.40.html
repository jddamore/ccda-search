Planned Encounter (V2) [2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.40, release 2014-06-09] view pdf

Description:

This template represents a planned or ordered encounter. The type of encounter (e.g., comprehensive outpatient visit) is represented. Clinicians participating in the encounter and the location of the planned encounter may be captured. The priority that the patient and providers place on the encounter may be represented.

Conformance Statements:

  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @classCode="ENC" (CodeSystem: HL7ActClass 2.16.840.1.113883.5.6 STATIC) (CONF:1098-8564).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @moodCode, which SHALL be selected from ValueSet Planned moodCode (Act/Encounter/Procedure) 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.23 STATIC 2014-09-01 (CONF:1098-8565).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] templateId (CONF:1098-30437) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @root="2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.40" (CONF:1098-30438).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @extension="2014-06-09" (CONF:1098-32553).
  • SHALL contain at least one [1..*] id (CONF:1098-8567).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] code, which SHOULD be selected from ValueSet Encounter Planned 2.16.840.1.113883.11.20.9.52 DYNAMIC (CONF:1098-31032).
  • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] statusCode (CONF:1098-30439).
    • This statusCode SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @code="active" Active (CodeSystem: HL7ActStatus 2.16.840.1.113883.5.14) (CONF:1098-31880).
  • SHOULD contain zero or one [0..1] effectiveTime (CONF:1098-30440).
  • MAY contain zero or more [0..*] performer (CONF:1098-30442) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] assignedEntity (CONF:1098-31874).
  • SHOULD contain zero or more [0..*] Author Participation (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.119) (CONF:1098-32045).
  • MAY contain zero or more [0..*] participant (CONF:1098-30443) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="LOC" Location (CodeSystem: HL7ParticipationType 2.16.840.1.113883.5.90) (CONF:1098-31875).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Service Delivery Location (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.32) (CONF:1098-31876).
  • MAY contain zero or one [0..1] entryRelationship (CONF:1098-31033) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="REFR" Refers to (CodeSystem: HL7ActRelationshipType 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1002) (CONF:1098-31034).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Priority Preference (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.143) (CONF:1098-31035).
  • MAY contain zero or more [0..*] entryRelationship (CONF:1098-31877) such that it
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] @typeCode="RSON" Has Reason (CodeSystem: HL7ActRelationshipType 2.16.840.1.113883.5.1002) (CONF:1098-31878).
    • SHALL contain exactly one [1..1] Indication (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.19) (CONF:1098-31879).
