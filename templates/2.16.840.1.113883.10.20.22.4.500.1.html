Care Team Member Act [act, 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.500.1, release 2019-07-01, open] view pdf

Description:

This template is used to represent a member of the care team. Care team members can include healthcare and community services providers, caregivers, relatives, the patient themselves, etc. A care team member can be another care team or an organization.Care team member attributes include the following:?tCare team member status on the care team?tCare team member time (e.g. duration, point-in-time, etc.) on the care team?tSchedule of the care team member describing when the care team member usually participates on the care team?tCare team member function on the care team such as the care team member specialty, relationship to the patient, and also role on the care team?tCare team member name, address, telecom, organization, etc.?tCare team member information (narrative description about the care team member)
