Non-Medicinal Supply Activity (V2) [supply, 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.50, release 2014-06-09, open] view pdf

Description:

This template represents equipment supplied to the patient (e.g., pumps, inhalers, wheelchairs). Devices applied to, or placed in, the patient are represented with the Product Instance entry contained within a Procedure Activity Procedure (V2) (identifier: 2.16.840.1.113883.10.20.22.4.14)
